About Me

Get to know who I am and what drives me

Hey there! I'm Barra Akmal, a high school student with a deep passion for technology, psychology, and making a positive impact on the world around me.

From a young age, I've been fascinated by how things work — whether it's a computer program, a psychological theory, or a social problem that needs solving. This curiosity has driven me to explore diverse fields and develop a unique interdisciplinary perspective.

When I'm not coding or studying psychology, you'll find me participating in student council debates, volunteering at community events, or contributing to the school's Computer Science Club as Vice President.

My long-term goal is to become an engineer who combines technical expertise with a deep understanding of human behavior to create solutions that truly make a difference in people's lives.
